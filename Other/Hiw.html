    How It Works

    Identify your item and model among the categories, select specifications, condition and accessories included to get a precise and instant price quote.

    Select your device from the Categories under Get Started. Choose accurate details of your gadget including specifications, condition and accessories provided. Based on your input, a price offer will be displayed instantly. Is your gadget not listed? Send details using Request a Quote and we shall send you an offer.

    Accept Price Quote, print shipping label and pack your product. It will be picked up from most locations in India free of cost and shipped to us for inspection.

    After accepting the Price Quote, complete the Check Out process and you will have an option to Print Shipping Label. The device needs to be packed securely to avoid damage and the shipping label to be affixed on the packing box. Shipment pickup will be arranged free of cost from most locations in India after coordinating with you.

    Once the product is checked at our end, payment will be sent to you by online bank transfer or cheque within 7 working days of receipt of your device.

    On receipt of the product, it will be inspected as per order after which payment will be sent. An honest and accurate description of your product enables quick payment. In case it differs from the details provided by you, your transaction may be re-quoted. You will always have the option to accept the re-quote and the amount will be paid to you, or your product will be returned back to you. It’s completely risk free.
